Here's how Timey Zoney keeps your community connected.

Automatic Nicknames

Set your timezone once with `/timezone`, and the bot automatically adds your UTC offset (e.g., `(UTC-5)`) to your nickname.

Smart DST Handling

Timey Zoney tracks Daylight Saving Time changes for you, automatically updating your nickname when the clocks change.

Multi-Server Sync

Your timezone follows you. Join any other server with Timey Zoney, and your nickname is updated instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my timezone?

It's simple! Just type `/timezone` and start typing your city, country, or a major region (e.g., `America/New_York`, `Europe/London`, `Australia/Sydney`). The bot will give you options to choose from.

What permissions does the bot need?

Timey Zoney needs the "Manage Nicknames" permission to work its magic. It also needs standard permissions to read messages and use slash commands.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We only store your Discord User ID and your chosen timezone. We don't store messages or any other personal info. You can permanently delete all your data at any time using the `/timezone delete` command.

What happens if I change my own nickname?

The bot is smart enough to detect changes. If you manually change your nickname, the bot will re-apply the UTC offset to the end of your new nickname to keep it consistent.
